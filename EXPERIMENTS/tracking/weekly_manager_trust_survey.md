Weekly Manager Trust Survey

Purpose: Capture manager-level trust and willingness to expand automation scope.

Delivery: Weekly email or Slack post to participating managers with a short form link.

Questions (1–5 Likert, 1=Strongly disagree, 5=Strongly agree):
1) Automated assignments this week were helpful. [1-5]
2) I am comfortable expanding auto-assignments to additional low/medium risk categories. [1-5]
3) I experienced any safety or guest-impacting issue this week. [Yes/No] (If Yes, please explain)

Optional open feedback box: "Any other thoughts or concerns?"

Success metrics:
- Average score on Q1 >= 3.5 after 2 weeks
- % of managers with Q2 >=4 (willing to expand) >=25% after 4 weeks

Suggested message copy:
"Hi [Name], thanks for piloting RelayPoint. Please complete this 1-min trust survey to help us improve automated assignments: [survey link] — Demond"
